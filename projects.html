I Am Your Senator

This campaign was born at the NACA Student Government Institute. The idea was to help students recognize and connect with their senators through personalized flyers that included photos and QR codes. These QR codes linked to senators’ blogs, Senate pages, or emails, lowering barriers to communication and making feedback easier. The project was later adopted by the Public Relations Committee and Executive Council, becoming a permanent initiative for all senators.
